How You Can Help

Please send ACL samples to feedback@networkmom.net. If our tool does not analyze a specific ACL correctly, we would love to fix it. The more samples we get, the more accurate our tool will be and the more quickly we can implement new features!

We welcome ACL samples for platforms we are not yet supporting. IPv6 ACL samples, in particular, will help our future development.

If this tool helps you, please tell your friends or give us a positive review in the MacOS App Store.
