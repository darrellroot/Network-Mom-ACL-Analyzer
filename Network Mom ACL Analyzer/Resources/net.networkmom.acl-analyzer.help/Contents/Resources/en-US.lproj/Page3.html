ASA Limitations

In addition to the general limitations, Network Mom ACL Analyzer has many limitations regarding analyzing ASA ACLS. Here are _some_ of those limitations:

  • ACEs marked as inactive are not understood.

  • The “forward-reference enable” command, which lets you use object-groups which are further down in your configuration is not supported. Object-groups must precede their use to be analyzed.

  • Service object-groups are not supported.
